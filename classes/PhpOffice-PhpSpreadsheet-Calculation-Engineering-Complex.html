Converts real and imaginary coefficients into a complex number of the form x +/- yi or x +/- yj.

Excel Function: COMPLEX(realNumber,imaginary[,suffix])

Parameters

the real float coefficient of the complex number

$imaginary : mixed = 0.0

$suffix : mixed = 'i'

The character suffix for the imaginary component of the complex number. If omitted, the suffix is assumed to be "i".
